Stove Repair +

  • Burner Issues: Burners not lighting up or heating unevenly can disrupt your cooking. We troubleshoot and fix ignition problems and heating inconsistencies.
  • Temperature Control: If your stove can't maintain the correct temperature, we address faulty thermostats and control switches to ensure consistent cooking.
  • Electric Stove Malfunctions: From non-responsive touch controls to broken heating elements, our experts handle all electric stove issues.
  • Gas Leaks: Safety is our priority. We detect and repair gas leaks promptly, ensuring your stove operates safely.
  • Broken Knobs and Switches: Worn-out or broken knobs and switches are common issues we repair, restoring full functionality to your stove.
  • Strange Noises: Unusual sounds from your stove can indicate underlying issues, which we identify and resolve efficiently.

Wine-cooler Repair +

  • Temperature Fluctuations: Inconsistent temperatures can spoil your wine collection. We fix cooling system issues to maintain the perfect temperature.
  • No Cooling: If your wine cooler isn't cooling at all, we diagnose and repair compressor or thermostat problems.
  • Noise Issues: Loud or unusual noises from your wine cooler indicate potential malfunctions. We identify and fix the sources of noise.
  • Door Seals: Damaged door seals can cause temperature loss. We replace faulty seals to ensure airtight closure.
  • Electrical Problems: Faulty wiring or control board issues can prevent your cooler from working properly. Our technicians handle all electrical repairs.
  • Humidity Control: Proper humidity is crucial for wine storage. We repair humidity control systems to preserve your wine's quality.

Refrigerator Repair +

  • Not Cooling: A refrigerator that isn't cooling can lead to spoiled food. We fix issues with compressors, thermostats, and refrigerant levels.
  • Leaking Water: Water leaks inside or around your refrigerator can be caused by clogged defrost drains or faulty water lines. We address all leak sources.
  • Strange Noises: Unusual sounds can indicate problems with fans, motors, or compressors, which we diagnose and repair.
  • Ice Maker Malfunctions: If your ice maker isn't producing ice, we troubleshoot and fix issues with water supply and ice maker mechanisms.
  • Frost Build-Up: Excessive frost in your freezer can affect its efficiency. We repair defrost systems to prevent frost build-up.
  • Door Seal Issues: Damaged door seals can cause temperature loss. We replace seals to ensure your refrigerator maintains the proper temperature.

Washer Repair +

  • Not Spinning: If your washer isn't spinning, it can be due to a faulty motor or drive belt. We diagnose and fix the issue promptly.
  • Water Leaks: Water leaks during wash cycles can be caused by damaged hoses or gaskets. We identify and repair all leaks.
  • No Drainage: If your washer isn't draining properly, it could be a clogged pump or drain hose. We clear blockages and restore proper drainage.
  • No Power: A washer that won't turn on may have electrical issues. We troubleshoot and repair all power-related problems.
  • Unusual Noises: Loud or strange noises during operation can indicate mechanical problems, which we identify and resolve.
  • Error Codes: Modern washers display error codes for various issues. We interpret and fix the problems associated with these codes.

Range Repair +

  • Burners Not Heating: Issues with gas or electric burners not heating can disrupt cooking. We troubleshoot and repair burner issues.
  • Oven Not Heating: If your oven isn't reaching the set temperature, we fix faulty heating elements and thermostats.
  • Control Panel Problems: Malfunctioning control panels can prevent proper operation. We repair or replace faulty control boards.
  • Ignition Issues: Gas range ignition problems can make cooking difficult. We diagnose and repair faulty ignitors and gas lines.
  • Broken Knobs: Worn-out or broken knobs can make it hard to control your range. We replace and repair all control knobs.
  • Door Problems: Oven doors that don't close properly can affect cooking. We fix hinge and seal issues to ensure proper closure.

Garbage Disposal Repair +

  • Jamming: Garbage disposals often jam due to food debris. We clear jams and restore normal operation.
  • Leaking: Leaks from your garbage disposal can be caused by faulty seals or gaskets. We identify and fix all leaks.
  • No Power: If your garbage disposal won't turn on, it could be an electrical issue. We troubleshoot and repair all power problems.
  • Unusual Noises: Loud or strange noises can indicate internal problems, which we diagnose and repair.
  • Slow Draining: Slow drainage can be caused by clogs. We clear blockages and restore proper drainage.
  • Bad Odors: Persistent bad odors can indicate trapped food particles. We clean and deodorize your garbage disposal.
