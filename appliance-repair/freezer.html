FAQ


How quickly can you repair my appliance?

We strive to provide our customers with the fastest possible service. In most cases, our technicians can come to you within 24-48 hours after your request. The duration of the repair depends on the complexity of the problem, but we always aim to complete the work as quickly as possible.

What if my appliance is beyond repair?

If our diagnosis shows that your appliance is beyond repair, we will offer you alternative solutions. This may include replacing the device with a new one or recommendations for further actions. Our goal is to help you make the best choice for your home and budget.

What guarantees do you provide for your services?

We provide a guarantee on all our appliance repair services. If the same problems occur after the repair, we will ensure the issues are resolved. We are confident in the quality of our work and strive to ensure complete customer satisfaction.

Do I need to bring my appliance to your workshop?

In most cases, our technicians come to your home and perform the repair on-site. This saves you time and effort. If a complex repair is required that cannot be done on-site, we will arrange transportation of the appliance to our workshop.

How much does appliance repair cost?

The cost of repair depends on the type of appliance, the complexity of the malfunction, and the necessary parts. We provide a preliminary cost estimate after diagnosing the problem. Our goal is to offer you competitive prices and high-quality service.

Common Freezer Problems We Fix


Not Freezing Properly

If your freezer isn't keeping food frozen, we'll diagnose and fix issues with the thermostat, evaporator coils, or compressor to restore proper freezing.

Ice Buildup

Excessive ice buildup can be a problem. We address defrost system issues and ensure proper airflow to prevent ice accumulation.

Strange Noises

Unusual sounds can indicate a problem. We diagnose and fix noises by checking the fan motors, compressor, and evaporator coils to ensure smooth operation.

Water Leaks

Water pooling inside or outside the freezer is a common issue. We find and fix leaks by inspecting the defrost drain, water supply lines, and door seals.

Door Seal Problems

Poor sealing can lead to temperature loss and frost buildup. We repair or replace door seals for optimal efficiency, ensuring a tight seal to maintain the internal temperature.

Electrical Problems

We troubleshoot and repair issues with electrical components and wiring, including faulty control boards, sensors, and power cords.
